HOW TO DEPLOY TO NETLIFY
1) Log in to Netlify.
2) Choose "Add new site" or drag-and-drop deploy.
3) Upload the ZIP file OR upload the extracted folder.
4) Once the site is live, go to:
   Site configuration / Domain management
5) Add your custom domain:
   equipmentusaco.com
6) Follow Netlify's DNS instructions at your domain registrar.

HOW TO SEND FORM NOTIFICATIONS TO SALES@EQUIPMENTUSACO.COM
1) In Netlify, open your deployed site.
2) Go to Forms.
3) Click each detected form.
4) Set email notifications to:
   sales@equipmentusaco.com
5) Submit a test form from the live site.
